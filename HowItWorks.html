About Serveo

Serveo is Kenya's premier platform connecting customers with trusted service professionals. Founded in 2023, our mission is to make finding reliable help for your home and business needs simple, secure, and efficient.

We carefully vet all service providers on our platform to ensure they meet our high standards for quality, reliability, and professionalism. Every provider undergoes background checks and verification processes before they can offer services through Serveo.

Whether you need an electrician, plumber, cleaner, mover, or any other service professional, Serveo provides a seamless way to find, book, and pay for services with complete peace of mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Booking a service is simple! Search for the service you need, select a provider that matches your requirements, choose a convenient time slot, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation with all the details.

How are service providers vetted?

All service providers undergo a rigorous verification process that includes background checks, license verification (where applicable), skills assessment, and review of previous work history. We also monitor customer ratings and reviews continuously.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept M-Pesa for mobile payments, as well as major credit and debit cards including Visa and Mastercard. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If you're not happy with the service provided, contact us within 24 hours and we'll work with the service provider to make it right or provide a refund according to our terms of service.

Can I schedule services in advance?

Yes! You can book services up to 30 days in advance. This is especially useful for planning larger projects or regular maintenance services.
