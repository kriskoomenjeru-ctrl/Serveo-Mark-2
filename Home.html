About Serveo

Serveo is Kenya's leading platform for connecting users with trusted service professionals. Our mission is to make finding reliable help for your home and business needs simple, secure, and efficient.

Founded in 2023, we've quickly grown to become the go-to platform for Kenyans seeking quality services from verified professionals. Every service provider on our platform undergoes a rigorous verification process to ensure they meet our high standards.

Whether you need an electrician, plumber, cleaner, mover, or any other service professional, Serveo provides a seamless way to find, book, and pay for services with complete peace of mind.
